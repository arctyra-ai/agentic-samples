# Business Plan Generator

AI-powered business plan generator built with a multi-agent development pipeline.

## Overview

A standalone web application (localhost) that guides a user through generating a professional, multi-section business plan using the LLM of their choice. Eight sequential pipeline stages produce market research, a services portfolio, and a complete business plan with editorial QA.

## Features

- Multi-provider LLM support: Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT), Google (Gemini), OpenRouter, and custom OpenAI-compatible endpoints
- Six-step business information wizard with per-field AI assistance
- Research-refinable fields: leave competitors, differentiators, and revenue goals blank and let the research phase inform them
- Two execution modes: step-by-step (review each stage) or full-auto
- Real-time token streaming with per-stage token usage and cost tracking
- Export to Microsoft Word (.docx) or Markdown (.md)
- Runs entirely on localhost — API keys are never saved to disk

## Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Role | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Research | Researcher | Market analysis, client challenges, competitive landscape |
| 2 — Research Synthesis | Researcher | Positioning strategy and ideal client profile |
| *Research Review* | *User* | *Review AI suggestions for fields left blank in wizard* |
| 3 — Services Draft | Writer | Full services menu with pricing |
| 4 — Services Review | Editor | Clarity, pricing accuracy, differentiation review |
| 5 — Services Final | Writer | Revised services incorporating editorial notes |
| 6 — Business Plan Draft | Writer | Full plan across all sections |
| 7 — Final QA | Editor | Five-dimension review with numbered fix list |
| 8 — Final Assembly | Writer | QA fixes incorporated, publish-ready document |
